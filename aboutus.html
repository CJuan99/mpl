About Us

We have moved to Wisma HELP, Bukit Damansara

The Malaysian Public Library (MPL) is a library currently located in Kampung Attap, KL. This library will soon be relocating to Wisma HELP, Bukit Damansara where all are welcomed to access the library. MPL was established in 1995 to encourage reading amongst the community. In addition, it also aims to preserve the Chinese culture and heritage. MPL has over 60,000 books, mostly in Chinese which include classical and modern literature.
